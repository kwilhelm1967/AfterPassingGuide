Security Overview

AfterPassing Guide

AfterPassing Guide is designed with one principle above all others: your information stays with you.

This application is built to function entirely offline, without accounts, cloud storage, or background data collection.

Data Location

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • Nothing is uploaded to external servers
  • No cloud backups, sync services, or third-party storage
  • No remote access by us or anyone else

Your information never leaves your device unless you explicitly export it.

Encryption

  • All stored data is encrypted at rest
  • Encryption is applied locally on your device
  • Access to data requires your device and your credentials
  • Exported files are generated only when you initiate them

There is no plaintext storage of sensitive information within the application.

Offline by Design

  • Works completely offline
  • No internet connection required to use the product
  • No background network activity
  • No automatic updates that transmit data

Connectivity is not required for daily use.

No Accounts. No Tracking.

  • No user accounts
  • No analytics
  • No telemetry
  • No behavioral tracking
  • No usage monitoring

We do not know how you use the product, what you store, or what actions you take inside it.

Device-Bound Use

  • Data is tied to the device where the application is installed
  • There is no server-side recovery of data
  • You are responsible for local device security and backups

This design prioritizes privacy and control over convenience.

Exports and Sharing

  • You control when and what is exported
  • Exports are generated locally on your device
  • Common export formats include PDF for estate and administrative handoff
  • Once exported, files are under your control and responsibility

AfterPassing Guide does not track or restrict how exported files are used.

What We Do Not Do

To be explicit, AfterPassing Guide does not:

  • Store your data in the cloud
  • Sync data across devices
  • Collect usage statistics
  • Share information with third parties
  • Provide remote access or recovery

Your Responsibility

Because this is a fully local product:

  • You are responsible for device security
  • You are responsible for backups if desired
  • Lost devices or deleted data cannot be recovered by us

This is a deliberate tradeoff made in favor of privacy.

Scope of the Product

AfterPassing Guide provides administrative guidance only. It does not provide legal, financial, tax, or medical advice.

Guidance is based on common U.S. administrative processes and may vary by state or situation. Always consult qualified professionals for decisions requiring expert judgment.
