Privacy Policy

Your privacy is protected by design, not by policy.

Effective Date: January 1, 2025

The Short Version: Local Aftercare Vault is designed so that we never have access to your data. All information is stored locally on your device. We cannot read, access, or sell your data because we never have it.

Information We Do Not Collect

Because Local Aftercare Vault operates entirely offline on your device, we do not collect:

  • Any information you enter into the application (tasks, documents, contacts, notes)
  • Personal information about you or your family
  • Information about the deceased person
  • Usage data or analytics from the application
  • Device fingerprints or identifiers (except for license activation)
  • Location data
  • Any telemetry or tracking information
  • Network traffic or communication data

Information We May Collect

The only information we may collect is:

Purchase Information

When you purchase a license, we receive your email address and payment confirmation from our payment processor (Stripe). This is used solely to deliver your license key and provide purchase support.

License Activation

During license activation, we receive your license key and a device fingerprint (a hash of your device's hardware characteristics). This is used solely to bind your license to your device. The device fingerprint cannot be used to identify you personally.

Support Communications

If you contact us for support, we retain our email correspondence to provide effective assistance. This includes your email address and the content of your messages.

How Your Data is Protected

All data you enter into Local Aftercare Vault is stored locally on your device using your operating system's standard storage mechanisms. We do not encrypt this data ourselves because it never leaves your device. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. The only sharing that occurs is:

  • Payment processing through Stripe (for purchases)
  • Email delivery services (for license keys and support)
  • License server (for activation - only license key and device hash, no personal data)

Embedded Mode

When Local Aftercare Vault runs as an add-on within Local Legacy Vault, it may access information stored in your Local Legacy Vault. This access is read-only and occurs entirely on your local device. No data is transmitted to us or any third party.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Request deletion of any email or purchase records we hold
  • Unsubscribe from any email communications
  • Request a copy of any data we have about you
  • Delete all application data by uninstalling the application

Children's Privacy

Local Aftercare Vault is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.

Contact

If you have questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at support@localvaultsoftware.com.
